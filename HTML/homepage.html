What I Do (and What I Want To Do)

I currently work at Ola Cabs, an app-based ride hailing service. Ola is a very rapidly growing company and the work is challenging at times, but the freedom to experiment and learn from my failures is what interests me and keeps me motivated.

On paper, I am part of the Operations team. But being the diverse and ever-changing company Ola is, I am continously allotted new projects and opportunites. It involves a lot of planning, running queries, analyzing results and executing ideas and then planning once again.


Going ahead I want to be in a tech-inclined role as an analyst or a software developer. Coding never tires me (apart from the seldom incessant bugs). Technology is ever-changing and I would like to be part of that change.
